Neon Bills —
Short Film

Set in a crime washed and corrupt district, Wyatt, an honest 20-year-old man is struggling to juggle his mother’s medical expenses and home’s overpriced rent. As long-term tenants, the two of them are threatened with eviction as their shortcomings begin to pile up. Relying on his job at a butcher shop, they manage to float above the water. Their landlord forcing them to pay up or get out, the tight rope Wyatt’s been walking is about to snap. About to lose everything financially, Wyatt searches for other possible means to pay off their debt. His boss offers a much more nefarious option and reality washes over him like a punch to the face. With what must be done in order to save the life he knew; Wyatt’s morals are stretched like a thread pressed to a razors edge.

Stubborn Love —
Short Film

A young woman named Julia struggles with modern day dating apps to find love. Her friend Chanel makes her an account and finds her a match with Vanessa. With different views on how the relationship should be, Vanessa realizes they aren’t good for each other and leaves her. Julia heartbroken goes home to find Chanel who comforts her. Chanel confesses her love for Vanessa and they embrace as Julia finally finds the love she has been looking for.
